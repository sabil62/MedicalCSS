ENDEMIC IN REGIONS

Mountain Region

There are many mountain in Nepal some of them are among the worlds highet peak which includes my everest the highest peak of the world kanchenjunga the third highest and dhaulagiri. Containing most of the peaks above 7000m tall, the life in this part of country is very hard. Climate and geography is the main challenge for survival, with less vegetation and arid land adding more to difficulty in the region.Tourism is one of the economic factor, other being collection of herbs.

Terai Region

Terai region is fertile south lying plains of nepal situated in indo gangetic plain.It is also called food granary of nepal as it contains most of the fertile regions of nepal and has the greatest population. The region is in border with india which inturn gives access to sea, Due to plain landscape and abundant fertile land, life is easier than other regions of country. More than half of all country's population lives here.
